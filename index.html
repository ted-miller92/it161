A little about me!

After years of slinging salads, toasting toast and roasting roasts, I have turned away from the life of late nights cooking toward a new life of late nights coding.

I love live music, travelling and socialising, just as anyone else, but this year away from such activitites has given me reason to develop my skills in something I can offer from from a point of physical isolation.The world of web development and networking offers possibilities for me to share my life, experience and skills from home or anywhere that I find myself in front of a computer.

The small amount of time I've spent learning and exploring this world has piqued my interest in web design and programming, and a little network management as well.
